The Best Movies According to Rob

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

Fight Club

I like this movie because of the ending twist that I was not able to predict. I also love the idea of standing up against corporate greed.

The Prestige

Another movie that did not let me know its secret until the very end. It is also a movie that show how people who are passionate about anything in their life can achieve great things if they give in to their passion.

V for Vendetta

A revolution movie. It got my attention just by the line "People should not be afraid of their Government. Their Government should be afraid of their people" alone.
